About My Role

As a key contributor to the server-side development of Algogauge, my role primarily involved setting up and managing the project's database, developing various controllers, working on communication between the front and back end, and working on the models. Although my main focus was backend development, I also had the opportunity to contribute to the front-end, specifically in designing and implementing the results pages.

Server-Side Contributions

  • Designed and developed robust models for storing the user's prior runs.

Client-Side Contributions

I also played a part in developing the front-end, particularly the results pages. This involved creating a table to display the resutls and adding pagination.
